Persian Paradise is an privately-owned Iranian cultural experience centre located in the FengTai district of Beijing. The award winning significant architecture is designed according to Persian traditional architectural principles.

  • Learn more

A space to experience genuine Persian culture

Enjoy Iranian architecture, taste Persian cuisine, Listen to Iranian music, try Iranian art, and buy original Iranian souvenirs; these are only a small part of the many rich experiences everyone can have in Persian Paradise's friendly space.

  • Experience programs

A window to cultural exchange

Persian Paradise is specifically designed and constructed to provide an international platform for cultural exchange of different nations. Multicultural art exhibitions and events help people from all around the world to know each other more and deeper.

  • Up coming events

A center for Intangible Cultural Heritage education

Various and productive educational programs have been planned for transferring valuable cultural heritage from previous generations to the younger generation in Persian Paradise. Experienced teachers and famous artists from different countries along the historical route of the Silk Road will help this center to achieve its goal.
